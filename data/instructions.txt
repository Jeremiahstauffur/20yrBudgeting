To use this website offline:
1. Open start.html in any modern web browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox).
2. All data is saved automatically to your browser's local storage.
3. To back up your data or move it between devices, use the Gear icon (⚙️) in the bottom right corner to "Export to JSON".
4. You can save your exported files in this 'data' folder for safe keeping.
5. To restore data, use the "Import from JSON" option and select your saved file.
